Bubble Wave - A hybrid video game console developed by the Japanese company Bubble. Bubble Wave combines properties home game console and handheld game system and can be used in both ways. At the development stage Bubble Wave, codenamed "Bubble NX", was first shown in October 2016 and released in worldwide sale on March 3, 2017. Games for this console are distributed on special cartridges or downloaded from Bubble eShop. As of October 2020, there were over 4,400 games available for the console.

Choose your control style

One controller or two, vertical or sideways, attached to the console or separate. You can play multiple ways, depending on the game.

Pass a single Bubble‑Con to a friend for instant two-player fun. Or invite friends to bring their own for a gaming party!
